Problem Statement: Rural Mobility Left Behind

In many rural and underserved areas of Ghana, transportation is unreliable, unsafe, and informal—especially with the widespread use of unregulated okadas (motorbike taxis). This lack of structure leads to frequent accidents, inconsistent pricing, and limited access to essential services, leaving communities disconnected and vulnerable.

Solution: Built for People, not Cars

ORDA Rides formalizes rural mobility by partnering with okada riders and local businesses to offer verified profiles, safety training, transparent pricing, and instant payouts. It combines ride-hailing, delivery, and hyper-local news into one lightweight platform, turning informal work into dignified livelihoods.

Vision: Serving where Mainstream Doesn't

We envision a Ghana where rural communities are fully included in the mobility economy—no longer left behind by mainstream transport systems. ORDA RIDES exists to make safe, reliable, and dignified transportation accessible to every town, no matter how small.

Impact: Building a sustainable future

By formalizing rural mobility, ORDA Rides reduces accidents, increases safety, and creates a sustainable livelihood for okada riders. It also provides a reliable transport option for communities, fostering economic growth and community cohesion.

More Questions?
Q&A
How do I book a ride with ORDA RIDES?

You can use the app, USSD, or SMS:

App: Open → enter pickup & destination → choose vehicle → confirm price → request.

USSD: Dial the ORDA short code → follow prompts → confirm request.

SMS: Text "Ride: From [pickup] to [destination]" → reply to confirm.

What types of vehicles are available?

Options include bikes, tricycles, and cars—depending on your location and needs.

Can I schedule a ride or delivery in advance?

Yes, scheduling is supported through the app.

How do I send a package using ORDA RIDES?

Use the delivery option in the app or via USSD/SMS. Include pickup and drop-off details.

What payment options are supported?

Mobile Money is the primary method, with instant payouts and settlements.

Is ORDA RIDES available offline?

Yes—USSD and SMS booking options ensure access even without internet.

How are drivers and riders verified?

All riders are verified with OTP handoff, photo proof, and safety training.

Can I track my ride or delivery in real time?

Yes, live tracking is built into the app for peace of mind.

How does ORDA RIDES keep me informed about local news?

The app includes a hyper-local news feed tailored to your community.

How can I become a driver or delivery rider with ORDA RIDES?

Apply via the app or website. Benefits include flexible hours, instant payouts, and safety support.
